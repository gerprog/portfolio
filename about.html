Hello!

Thanks for taking a look at my work.

This portfolio displays examples of mostly college projects that showcase my tech and design skills. I relish working with a good team but also those projects where I've had to fly solo.

I hold a Masters in Web Technologies from National College of Ireland (honours graduate 9/2014). I'm living in Dublin docklands.

I have very exacting standards as I work. Having worked as a designer for print previous to returning to college, my eye for detail was pretty meticulous before I started to code. Now it has sharpened all the more.

My dissertation focused on e-learning, and this is an area I am eager to explore further. I am currently studying Mobile Technologies in a part time Post Grad Diploma at NCI, two evenings a week. I hope to soon develop mobile e-learning apps and more.

When I'm away from the desk my interests are focused on creativity in many fields. Art, design, photography, a great gig, an engrossing read with good coffee, and cycling.

Thanks again, and feel free to get in touch.
