Print

I worked for 12 years as a designer with Mahons Printing Works, a long established Dublin printing company. The work was created using the AdobeCS and QuarkXpress.

I designed for a wide range of public and private sector clients eg. Stonehouse Wholesale, Mace, the Kings Inns, Law Reform Commission, CPSU.
Trade publications, book covers, annual reports, poster work, periodicals etc.

At Mahons I developed a very high attention to detail. I worked at speed, always within deadline, and enjoyed guiding customers through the design process to the finished product.

Technology used:

Adobe InDesign
Adobe Photoshop
Adobe Illustrator
QuarkXpress

The Stonehouse group of wholesale grocery retailers and suppliers required a monthly booklet for current offers.

The cover was a chance to indulge in a lot of Photoshop gradients and filters popular at the time.

Some book cover designs.

I would often typeset the contents in QuarkXpress or InDesign, and sharpened my eye for detail while proof reading.
