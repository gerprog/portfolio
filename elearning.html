e-learning episodes

This application was used to explore 3 e-learning epistemologies (theories) for my MSc Web Technologies dissertation "A critical analysis of e-learning epistemologies using the EBI and VARK frameworks". These are Objectivism in Quiz 1, Constructivism in Quiz 2, and Connectivism in Quiz 3.

A small study group was eye-tracked and questioned to gather both quantitative and qualitative data on motivation and engagement levels. Of those tested, Connectivism was found to be the preferred epistemology.

Technology used:

HTML5, CSS3, Adobe CS.
JavaScript and JQuery for the quiz functionality.
JQueryUI for the drag and drop functionality.
AngularJS for the display of video channels.
Various plugins for the chatroom, the forum, the share button, and the AngularJS notepad. Bootstrap.
